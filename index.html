№ 07 — Questions

The ones
we hear first.

Don't see yours? Email [email protected] and we'll answer straight.

How long does a project actually take? +

Most builds go live 3–4 weeks after kickoff. Starter Presence can be as fast as 7 days. We move quickly because we only take on a few projects at a time.

Do I have to write the copy? +

No. We write the entire site for you based on a 30-minute call and your existing reviews. You review, tweak, approve. That's it.

Will my site actually show up on Google? +

Every site ships with a proper local SEO foundation: schema, metadata, service area pages, and a Google Business Profile check. Growth tier clients get ongoing work to keep climbing.

What happens if I want to leave later? +

You own everything. Your domain stays in your account, your content is yours, and we'll help you migrate hosting if you ever want to. No games.

Do you only work with Houston businesses? +

Houston and Texas are our home base and where we do most of our work. We take select projects outside Texas when the fit is right.
